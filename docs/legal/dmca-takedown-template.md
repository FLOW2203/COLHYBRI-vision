# Takedown notice templates — ONLYMORE Group

Two ready-to-send templates: **DMCA** (17 U.S.C. § 512 — most global
platforms) and **LCEN** (Loi n° 2004-575 art. 6 — French hosts and
sites). Fill in the four `[BRACKETS]` fields and send from
`onlymore2024@gmail.com`.

Attach:
1. Screenshot of the original content on `colhybri.vision` or
   `onlymore.group` with timestamp.
2. Screenshot of the infringing content with full URL visible.
3. Proof of copyright: footer © notice + EXIF metadata (run
   `scripts/embed-copyright-metadata.sh` first).
4. Proof of identity (ID / KBIS excerpt for ONLYMORE Group).

Typical resolution times:
- Major platforms (Google / Meta / YouTube / X): 24–72h.
- Independent site / small host: 7–14 days with follow-up.
- Escalation path: host abuse desk → national cybercrime authority
  (Pharos in France, IC3 in the US).
